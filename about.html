About me

I work on research that critically examines the role of design in digital technology, specifically in areas such as privacy, accessibility, and digital identities. My areas of interest include design studies and digital cultures. I am a communication design graduate from the National Institute of Fashion Technology, New Delhi and I have a masters in social design from Ambedkar University Delhi. I have previously worked as a senior design researcher at the Centre for Internet and Society, Bangalore.
